account


## phrase

think of & think about

### think about

1.take someone or something into account or consideration when deciding on a possible action.

"you can live how you like, but there's the children to think about"

2.consider the possibility or advantages of a course of action.

"she thought about enrolling for a college course"

consider/contemplate



### think of

phrasal verb of think

have a specified opinion of something.

"she did not think highly of modern art"

take something into account or consideration when deciding on a possible action.

"think of the pain your family is going to feel"

consider the possibility or advantages of a course of action.

"he was thinking of becoming a zoologist"

used to indicate that a course of action is too unlikely or undesirable to be considered a possibility.

"I couldn't think of letting you take the blame"

call something to mind; remember.

"lemon thyme is a natural pair with any chicken dish you can think of"

recall/remember/recollect
